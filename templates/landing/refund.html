Cancellation, Refund & Replacement Policy

Last updated: {% now "F Y" %}

At THELASTCARD, each product is customized based on the information provided by the customer.

cancel Order Cancellation

Orders can be cancelled within 24 hours of placing the order. After this period, production may already have started and the order cannot be cancelled.

design_services Customized Product Policy

THELASTCARD products are personalized and customized. Customized products cannot be returned or refunded unless there is a manufacturing defect or an error from our side.

swap_horiz Replacement Policy

We will offer a replacement free of cost in the following cases:

  • The card arrives damaged
  • The NFC functionality is not working
  • Incorrect details were printed due to our error

Customers must provide clear photo or video proof of the issue.

schedule Replacement Request Window

Replacement requests must be submitted within 7 days of receiving the order.

block Non-Eligible Cases

Replacement or refund requests may not be accepted if:

  • Incorrect information was provided by the customer
  • Damage occurred due to misuse or mishandling

email Support Contact

For any cancellation, refund, or replacement queries, please reach out to us at support@thelastcard.in
