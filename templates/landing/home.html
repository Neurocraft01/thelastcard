FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a monthly subscription fee?

expand_more

No! The price you see is a one-time payment for your physical card. Basic digital profile features are included forever with no recurring fees.

Can I update my information after purchase?

expand_more

Yes, you can update your digital profile unlimited times without ordering a new card. That's the beauty of NFC technology!

Do recipients need an app to view my card?

expand_more

No app needed! When someone taps your card, your profile opens directly in their phone's web browser. It works on all modern smartphones.

Will this work on all phones?

expand_more

Absolutely. The Last Card supports both NFC tap and QR scan. Even if the other person's phone doesn't have NFC, they can scan the QR code and access your details immediately.

Which technology does The Last Card use?

expand_more

The Last Card uses a combination of NFC (Near Field Communication) and QR code technology. You can share your profile instantly by tapping the card on NFC-enabled phones or by scanning the QR code on any smartphone.

What's the difference between Matte and Gloss?

expand_more

Matte finish has a smooth, non-reflective surface that feels premium. Gloss finish has a high-shine, reflective coating that makes colors pop and adds a luxury feel.

How long does shipping take?

expand_more

Standard shipping is 5-7 business days. Express shipping (2-3 days) is available at checkout for an additional fee.

What information can I share with my card?

expand_more

You can share your contact info, social media handles, website, portfolio, and more - all in a single tap using 'THELASTCARD'.
